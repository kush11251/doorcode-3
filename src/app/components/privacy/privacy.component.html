1. Data Collection & Usage

At DoorCode, we orchestrate exceptional events through AI-driven logistics. To provide our secure check-in services, we collect minimal necessary data, including email addresses, event metadata, and real-time scanning analytics. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Secure DoorCodes & Tracking

Our platform utilizes encrypted, time-sensitive access codes. When these codes are scanned at an event, we log the timestamp and entry point to populate real-time analytics for the organizer. This data is aggregated and anonymized after the event concludes.

3. Data Retention

Event attendee lists and basic profile information are retained for as long as the organizer maintains an active account. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting our support team.
